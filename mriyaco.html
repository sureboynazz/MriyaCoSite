WHO ARE WE



Mriya Co Limited is a Nigerian-based company that provides a range of Multi-Disciplinary Services. The company's services are based on its Core Strengths, Expertise, and Extensive Local and International Network. The company is committed to Effective Communication and is passionate about perfecting the way people Connect, Communicate, Share Ideas, and Retain Knowledge on a daily basis.

Mriya Co Limited utilizes audio-visual technology solutions to simplify the process of communicating with peers, colleagues, mentors, and students. The company strives to explore and implement new creative ways that enhance all aspects of the daily huddle, weekly all-hands, or monthly board meeting. The company believes that these creative and innovative audio-visual solutions should be readily available to everyone, regardless of age, size, or industry.Mriya Co Limited transforms workspaces into destinations by creating seamless Hybrid Meeting Experiences that maintain the Digital Workflow for those working remotely, at a desk in an office, or in a meeting room. The company takes the time to understand the client's business and design collaboration solutions for the needs of every space and user requirement. The company is a specialist in Microsoft Team Rooms and Team certified devices, and it consults, procures certified Team Rooms devices and licenses, it deploys, and ensures user adoption through comprehensive training.

SOFTWARE PLATFORMS WE INTERGRATE

Google Meet

Google Meet is a video conferencing app suitable for businesses of all sizes. The app allows users to join pre-scheduled meetings from calendar events, choose a link, enter a meeting code, or even dial in from their phones.

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is a hub for office team collaboration. A unified, cloud-based communication platform that enables group chat, online meetings, and video conferencing.

Zoom

Zoom integrates cloud video conferencing, online meetings, and group messaging into one easy-to-use platform. Having unified compatibility between Android, iOS, PC and Mac devices makes the user experience seamless and accessible for all those around the world
